How is Breaktime different to other focus apps?

We believe that starting a focus period manually each time isn't the right approach. Why not block apps permanently and start a break period to unlock it?

Default ScreenTime statistics are flawed. Not all ScreenTime is unproductive. Breaktime tracks your distraction time accurately.

Overcomplicated user interfaces with excessive features are distracting (defeating the purpose), we keep it simple to help you stay focused.
